About

"The Academic Calendar" hosts an interactive web-application which allows students to keep track of their academic progress through a semester. Created primarily for the members of Triangle Fraternity (Illinois Chapter).

The idea for the Academic Calendar was originally conceived by SJW via an Excel Spreadsheet.

Built from the Python Flask Framework, Bootstrap, D3, and MySQL by DDP.
